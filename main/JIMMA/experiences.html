Experiences

Art & Craft

Immerse yourself in the vibrant arts and crafts scene of Jimma, where local artisans specialize in exquisite woodwork. Explore the bustling markets and discover intricately carved wooden sculptures, masks, and decorative objects. Engage with the skilled craftsmen, learn about their traditional techniques, and witness firsthand the artistry that has made Jimma renowned for its woodwork. Take home a unique piece that captures the essence of Jimma's rich artistic heritage.

Experience the vibrant spirit of Jimma through its exciting events and festivals. Immerse yourself in the joyous atmosphere of Timket, the Ethiopian Epiphany, with colorful processions and water baptisms. Don't miss the Meskel festival in September, filled with bonfires, processions, and traditional ceremonies. Additionally, witness the event where elders showcase their strongest horses, adding a touch of equestrian excellence to the festivities. These celebrations offer a unique opportunity to engage with the local community and celebrate the rich traditions of Jimma.

Nature

Jimma boasts lush forests, picturesque rivers, and stunning waterfalls. Explore the tranquil trails of Jimma Forest, witness the graceful flow of the Kito River, and marvel at the cascading beauty of Biftu Waterfall. Whether seeking adventure or relaxation, Jimma's natural wonders offer a sanctuary for exploration and rejuvenation.
